WELCOME TO ENKORE KARAOKE



Enkore Karaoke at Bellevue and Univerisity District offers our clients an entertainment venue for a truly private karaoke experience.

Our soundproofed rooms house state-of-the-art karaoke and audio/visual systems. Our rooms are designed to accomadate from up to 8 people or up to 20 people to meet your karaoke needs. Our song database includes 80K+ songs in Chinese, Korean, Japanese and English.

Unlike other karaoke, admission is charged per the minute, so enjoyment is of the focus rather than the time!
