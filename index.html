WELCOME TO ENKORE KARAOKE



Enkore Karaoke at Bellevue and Univerisity District offers our clients an entertainment venue for a truly private karaoke experience. Our soundproofed rooms house state-of-the-art karaoke and audio/visual systems. We rooms that can accommodate up to 8 people or up to 20 people. Our song database includes 80K+ songs in Chinese, Korean, Japanese and English. Unlike other karaoke, admission is charged on an minute basis.
