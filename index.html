Services

Enkore Seattle

Enkore Karaoke at Univerisity District offers our clients an entertainment venue for a truly private karaoke experience. Our 11 soundproofed rooms house state-of-the-art karaoke and audio/visual systems. We have 8 small rooms that can accommodate up to 8 people, 3 Large rooms for up to 15 people. We have 2 differenet karaoke systems for individual need.

Enkore Bellevue

Enkore Karaoke at Bellevue offers our clients an entertainment venue for a truly private karaoke experience. Our 10 soundproofed rooms house state-of-the-art karaoke and audio/visual systems. We have 7 small rooms that can accommodate up to 8 people, and 3 large rooms for up to 20+ people. Our song database includes 80K+ songs in Chinese, Korean, Japanese, and English. Admission is charged on minute to minute basis.
